# Home Wireframe — Sat Apps Marketplace

## 1. Purpose

The Home screen should immediately communicate:

- premium app marketplace
- modern business solutions
- strong visual presentation
- quick path to browse apps
- quick path to contact / start project

The screen should feel like a premium landing page, not a plain list screen.

---

## 2. Main Goal of the Home Screen

The Home screen should help users do these things fast:

1. understand what Sat Apps offers
2. feel the product quality visually
3. see featured apps
4. trust the brand
5. move to Apps, AppDetails, About, or Contact

---

## 3. Screen Flow Order

The Home screen should follow this order from top to bottom:

1. sticky top header
2. hero section
3. metrics strip
4. featured preview card
5. trust strip
6. premium highlight block
7. featured apps section
8. why choose us block
9. CTA block
10. common footer

---

## 4. Section-by-Section Wireframe

## A. Sticky Header

### Purpose
Always visible branding and top-level navigation.

### Content
- brand dot
- brand name: `App Marketplace` or `Sat Apps`
- active label: `Apps`
- compact action button: `Contact`

### Behavior
- sticky while scrolling
- slightly scales/shifts on scroll
- remains compact and clean

### Notes
- keep it premium and minimal
- do not overcrowd
- use glass dark top bar style

---

## B. Hero Section

### Purpose
This is the strongest first impression area.

### Content
- eyebrow text:
  - `PREMIUM APP MARKETPLACE`
- hero title:
  - strong multi-line title
- short subtitle
- two primary actions:
  - `Explore Apps`
  - `Contact Us`

### Layout
- left-aligned text
- bold headline
- generous vertical spacing
- action buttons below subtitle

### Notes
Hero should communicate:
- quality
- premium feel
- clear product/business value

---

## C. Metrics Strip

### Purpose
Show quick credibility and marketplace scale.

### Content
3 metrics:
- `20+ Apps`
- `10+ Industries`
- `100% Custom`

### Layout
- horizontal strip
- three equal blocks
- small divider lines between items

### Notes
Should sit directly below hero CTA buttons.

---

## D. Featured Preview Card

### Purpose
Visually show one featured app/product.

### Content
- large product image
- overlay content box
- chip: `Featured Product`
- product title
- short description

### Layout
- large wide card
- image background
- text overlay near bottom

### Notes
This is a premium visual anchor after the hero.

---

## E. Trust Strip

### Purpose
Quick confidence-building statements.

### Content
3 items:
- `Trusted / by growing teams`
- `10+ / Categories`
- `Fast / deployment`

### Layout
- compact horizontal strip
- centered items
- small dividers

### Notes
Should look lighter and more compact than the metrics strip.

---

## F. Premium Highlight Block

### Purpose
Promote the most important app/package.

### Content
- badge: `PREMIUM APP`
- mini pill: `Best Seller`
- featured title
- supporting text
- tags:
  - `Full Business Suite`
  - `Most Requested`
  - `Highly Customizable`
- premium price
- CTA:
  - `View Premium App`

### Layout
- larger highlighted card
- premium accent border
- text-heavy with structured spacing
- CTA aligned in bottom row

### Notes
This section should feel like a featured offer.

---

## G. Featured Apps Section

### Purpose
Show a preview of a few marketplace apps.

### Content
- section eyebrow: `FEATURED APPS`
- section title
- action link: `See all`
- horizontal cards for 3 apps

### Card Content
Each app card should contain:
- image
- category chip
- app title
- short description
- price
- small CTA button: `View`

### Layout
- horizontal scroll
- medium-sized cards
- enough spacing between cards

### Notes
This section should encourage moving into `AppsScreen`.

---

## H. Why Choose Us Block

### Purpose
Explain value in a short structured way.

### Content
- eyebrow: `WHY CHOOSE US`
- title
- 3 bullet/value rows:
  - premium presentation
  - stronger buyer confidence
  - ready for demos, pricing, scale

### Layout
- one large card block
- vertical list with dots/icons

### Notes
Readable, clean, business-focused.

---

## I. CTA Block

### Purpose
Push conversion near end of screen.

### Content
- eyebrow: `START YOUR SHOWCASE`
- title
- supporting paragraph
- buttons:
  - `Start Project`
  - `Browse Apps`

### Layout
- highlighted block
- strong visual contrast
- stacked buttons on mobile

### Notes
This is the main conversion section before footer.

---

## J. Common Footer

### Purpose
Provide brand, navigation, product links, support links, and contact details.

### Content
- brand section
- 4 columns:
  - Company
  - Products
  - Support
  - Contact
- copyright row
- privacy / terms mini row

### Notes
Must be reusable across:
- Home
- Apps
- AppDetails
- About
- Contact

---

## 5. Navigation Behavior

Home screen actions should route as follows:

- `Explore Apps` → `Apps`
- `Contact Us` → `Contact`
- `See all` → `Apps`
- `View` on app cards → `AppDetails`
- `View Premium App` → `AppDetails`
- `Start Project` → `Contact`
- `Browse Apps` → `Apps`

---

## 6. Visual Behavior

## Animations
Use soft entrance animations:
- fade up
- slight scale fade
- staggered card reveal
- subtle floating for preview section

## Scroll Behavior
- sticky header
- hero area fades/scales on scroll
- content below moves smoothly upward

## Press Behavior
- opacity slightly reduced
- slight scale down

---

## 7. Mobile Layout Notes

- all main sections stacked vertically
- horizontal scrolling only for featured apps
- text should never feel cramped
- keep large tap areas for buttons
- footer should wrap properly into rows/columns

---

## 8. Content Tone

Copy should sound:
- premium
- business-ready
- modern
- trustworthy
- conversion-focused

Avoid:
- overly casual language
- technical overload
- too much text in one section

---

## 9. Home Screen Success Criteria

The Home screen is successful if the user can immediately:

- understand what Sat Apps does
- feel that the brand is premium
- browse featured apps
- trust the business
- move to contact or app details easily
