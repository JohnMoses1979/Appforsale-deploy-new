# About Wireframe — Sat Apps Marketplace

## 1. Purpose

The About screen explains the company, builds trust, and supports the premium brand story.

It should:
- explain who Sat Apps is
- communicate mission and values
- strengthen credibility
- support conversion later

---

## 2. Main Goal

Users should be able to:
1. understand the company
2. trust the business more
3. see the quality mindset behind the marketplace
4. move to Contact if interested

---

## 3. Screen Flow Order

Top to bottom:

1. top bar
2. hero block
3. stats strip
4. story block
5. values block
6. mission / vision cards
7. CTA block
8. common footer

---

## 4. Section-by-Section Wireframe

## A. Top Bar

### Content
- back button
- title: `About Us`
- subtitle: `Who we are`
- contact action

### Purpose
- consistent page entry
- quick contact option

---

## B. Hero Block

### Content
- eyebrow: `ABOUT SAT APPS`
- strong headline
- short company summary
- supporting pills:
  - `Premium Apps`
  - `Custom Solutions`
  - `Web & Mobile`

### Purpose
- first brand impression for About page

---

## C. Stats Strip

### Content
- `20+ Solutions`
- `10+ Categories`
- `100% Custom Focus`

### Purpose
- quick credibility layer

---

## D. Story Block

### Content
- eyebrow: `OUR STORY`
- title
- 1–2 paragraphs

### Purpose
- explain the company in human/business terms
- communicate quality and product thinking

---

## E. Values Block

### Content
list of core values:
- Premium Product Thinking
- Custom Business Solutions
- Scalable Delivery
- Long-Term Support

### Purpose
- define brand principles clearly

---

## F. Mission / Vision Cards

### Card 1
- `MISSION`
- short explanation

### Card 2
- `VISION`
- short explanation

### Purpose
- structured brand messaging
- cleaner scannability

---

## G. CTA Block

### Content
- eyebrow
- title
- short support text
- buttons:
  - `Contact Us`
  - `Browse Apps`

### Purpose
- move users into action after trust is built

---

## H. Footer

Must use:
- `CommonFooter`

---

## 5. Success Criteria

The About screen succeeds if users:
- trust the brand more
- understand the company clearly
- feel the product quality mindset
- know what to do next
